Welcome to the Online Auction System

Steps for Buyers:

  1. Create an account by clicking on the "Register" button.
  2. Login to your account using your credentials.
  3. Browse the available auctions and select the items you want to bid on.
  4. Place your bids on the desired items.
  5. Monitor the auctions and adjust your bids as necessary.
  6. If you win an auction, follow the payment instructions provided.
  7. Coordinate with the seller for the delivery or pickup of the item.

Steps for Sellers:

  1. Create an account by clicking on the "Register" button.
  2. Login to your account using your credentials.
  3. Create an auction listing for the item you want to sell.
  4. Specify the starting bid, auction duration, and any other relevant details.
  5. Monitor the auction and communicate with potential buyers.
  6. Once the auction ends, contact the winning bidder and arrange for payment and delivery.
  7. Complete the transaction and provide feedback as appropriate.

How to Use:

To get started with the Online Auction System:

  1. Click on the "Register" button to create a new account.
  2. After registration, login using your credentials.
  3. Explore the available auctions or create your own if you're a seller.
  4. Bid on items, manage your auctions, and interact with other users as needed.
  5. Make sure to review the auction rules and guidelines for a smooth experience.
  6. If you have any questions or need assistance, please visit the "Contact Us" page.
